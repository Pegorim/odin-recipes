Eggnog Waffles

What is the Eggnog Waffle? Check out this description of it:

Hey there! Have you ever tried an eggnog waffle? It's like the holiday season wrapped up in breakfast! Eggnog, that sweet and creamy drink everyone sips around the holidays, is the secret ingredient. It's usually made with milk, cream, sugar, whipped eggs, and awesome spices like nutmeg and cinnamon.


So, what happens when you mix eggnog into waffle batter? You get this amazing, sweet, and spiced-up waffle! Instead of using regular milk or buttermilk, you use eggnog to give the waffles a super cool flavor twist.


These aren't just any waffles; they're super tender, a bit custardy inside, and they carry a hint of those classic eggnog spices. Imagine biting into a warm, fluffy waffle with all those holiday vibes! People love to top them with all sorts of things like syrup, whipped cream, or even fresh fruits.


Eggnog waffles are a big hit during the holidays. It's like having a bit of Christmas cheer for breakfast! So next time you're looking for a festive and yummy twist on your regular waffles, give eggnog waffles a try!

Here is what you need to make this delicious holiday treat:


Ingridients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups eggnog
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted

Step by step guide to make this recipe:

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl. Add eggnog, eggs, and butter; mix well.
  2. Preheat a waffle iron according to manufacturer's instructions. Pour 1/4 of the batter into the preheated iron and cook until waffle is crisp and golden, about 5 minutes. Repeat with remaining batter.
